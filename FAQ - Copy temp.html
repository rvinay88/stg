Frequently Asked Questions

All our items are genuine and directly featured through partnership with the designers and when an order is placed, you are buying directly from the brands themselves.

Before you start shopping, we recommend that you read our Terms and Conditions. Once you register on our website, you will be able to place an order.

You can choose to pay by credit card or debit card. runway2street accepts all major credit cards like Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. Some cards may require an authorization from the issuing bank. All payments are processed through a secure checkout system provided by Authorize.net.

You can add a credit card my clicking on your profile image on top of page and accessing My Accounts which will allow you to update all your preferences.

We currently offer ability to buy in USD ($). We will be adding support soon for additional currencies like UK Pound Sterling (£), US Dollar ($), Euro (€) or INR (₹). You can select the currency by selecting the respective currency symbol in the upper right hand side of the site. We will process the payment in your chosen currency, however, please be aware that if your bank account is in a different currency, the exchange rate used to debit your account will be determined by your bank / PayPal automatically, and not by runway2street.com.

When you are viewing a garment you can click on the size & fit tab next to description to get more details about the product. You also have a link for item measurements and What size am I for more details on how this product compares to others. Simply cross-check these measurements with your own to ensure you have ordered the correct size. For a specific size or measurement query please contact our Customer Service team who will be happy to assist you. If the item doesn't fit you, you can always return it, please refer to our Returns policy.

All items shown on the website are in stock. However, please note that adding an item to your shopping bag does not automatically reserve it for you, and that it can be purchased by another customer while you continue to shop for other items.

We are always on the lookout for new and exciting designers to add to our site. We also send newsletters to our customers to announce arrival of new designers or collections.

Please contact our Customer Service Team and we will be happy to assist you with details on whether a specific size will be available again or if we can pre-order it for you.

Special requests may be available depending on the product, please contact our Customer Service Team with the request. We are able to offer customizations through many of our designers.

We do offer a pre-order option for several products. You will see the pre-order option next to the item when we do. Please note that the item will be shipped after it is available in this case and duration varies from designer to designer. The estimated ship date is displayed when you choose pre-order.

We guarantee that all personal data will be kept private and confidential, and that we will never give out any of your personal data without your authorization. For further details check Our Privacy Policy.

After your purchase has been made you will receive a confirmation e-mail, which doesn't mean that your order has been processed by us. Once your credit card and address details have been approved and verified, an order confirmation email will be sent to you with the order number and details.

runway2street.com is happy to accept cancellations through our customer support, for any reason, up to the moment your order has been processed by our designers. After that, you cannot cancel your order, but you may return it if you wish.

Payment will be taken immediately, and you will receive an email confirming your order once all details have been approved. If for some reason we are unable to process the order, the amount will be refunded back to same credit or debit card.

Shipping costs will be calculated based on the size, weight and destination of your package. All shipments are processed by runway2street.com's partner carriers. The shipping charges are displayed on the order confirmation page. We work hard to subsidize these costs for you.

If you are a specific country and shopping from a designer outside that country, you will see prices inclusive of taxes and import duties.

All our packages are insured from theft and damage while in transit to your shipping address. However, it is not covered after the delivery at your address. We do require a signature for delivery of our shipments.

You will receive regular updates on your order, and you can also check the status in your My Account section on the website. To access My Accounts, click on your profile picture in top right corner. Then choose your order under My Orders and using the tracking link.

If you have ordered items from more than one designer in your order, then the items will arrive in separate shipments from each designer, and may not be delivered to you simultaneously. You will see individual tracking details in your My Orders section on the site. To access My Orders click on your profile picture in top right corner and then choose My Account. Or you can contact our our Customer Service Team who will be able to assist you.

We offer the most unique designer products on the web, which also means that quantities of each item in stock may be limited. We keep our web site stock synchronized with the designer’s stock. However, if the goods are sold at the designer’s store it may happen that the stock information is inaccurate.

We can only ship an order to one single address. When you place an order, you will be asked to choose one shipping address for your delivery. You can always choose to use a different address than on file.

Once your order and payment details have been verified and approved, we will ship your order using express service (default) within 3 business days. The transit time for express shipments is typically around 7-10 days.

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If for any reason, you are not satisfied with the item, you can return the items in original condition with all tags attached. The process is simple and easy to use but we do charge a nominal return shipping fee.

To use this service, you will need to request a Returns Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number within 5 days of receiving your order and book your free pick up within the next 5 days. See below for more detail

Sign in at runway2street.com, click on your profile picture on top right corner, to access My Account, select an order under My Orders, choose relevant items you wish to return with the reason code and click create return. You will then receive an RMA number to book your free collection. Please refer to our Returns and Refunds section for full instructions on returns.

Once you have received your Returns Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number, please ensure you book your pick up within 5 days of receiving it. Pack your items back in original box and fix the return shipping label on it. Please refer to our Returns and Refunds section for full instructions on returns.

Once your return has been received by the designer and it complies with our Returns Policy, we will refund you for the item returned by your original payment method. Please be aware that payments can take up to 14 working days to process depending on your bank or payment provider. Returned items will be refunded excluding the cost of shipping.

If a cancellation is made prior to order processing, and we have confirmed your order has been cancelled by email, then we will fully refund you through your original payment method depending on whether we already took payment from your account or not.

Customers will be refunded the cost of your order including any sales tax and import duties that were applicable at the time of your purchase but excluding the shipping costs.

If you would like to exchange an item, you will need to return the original item for which you will be refunded and then you can place a new order. If an item is unavailable please contact our Customer Service Team who might be able to pre-order or track it down for you.
