There is no question that German/Lebanese designer, Anja Najjar, exudes confidence. Through her bold yet classy designs, she delivers a yin and yang like balance of classic and current. Her style caters to a woman whose clothing complements her personality rather than merely enhancing her appearance.

Anja is fond of working with different original materials like natural cork, fabrics, textures, and innovative resources like chunky, gold-plated hardware which are incorporated into her pieces. runway2street is thrilled to feature handbags from the “anja” brand. These playful and vibrant pieces are a perfect way to accent any outfit.

Anja studied design in Lebanon at ESMOD (l'Ecole Supérieure des Arts et techniques de la Mode), after majoring in fine arts while living in Germany. After completing several internships, having worked for a classic German label and a designer in Beirut, Anja decided to launch her own brand.
