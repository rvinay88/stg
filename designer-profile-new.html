You can recognize a truly talented designer by their ability to create pieces beautiful enough to frame an entire outfit. That knack for elegance has us falling in love with Astrid Sarkissan’s newest collection of luxurious silk scarves.

Just one look at the collection will have you “ooh”-ing and “ahh”-ing over Astrid Sarkissan’s one-of-a-kind prints influenced by gothic elements, stained glass and animals, gorgeous enough to be hanging on museum walls.
